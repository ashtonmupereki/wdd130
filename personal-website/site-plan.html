Overview

Purpose

The major goal of the website is to make it simple for people to get food by providing daily updates on the meals, prices, and other special services that our Cosmic Daily Meal Restaurant offers to the public. No academic qualifications are needed and we do not take race into consideration also.

Audience

This website is reffered to as the hunger warrior for children. This indicates that we are able to provide excellent services to all children from 5years to 12years in the world at reasonable costs. 24hour assistance is provided. Children within the range of 5years and 12years can eat the clean, wholesome meal. Children are our target customer. Our site will be available on different platforms and children can accesss it on desktops, laptops and other devices. Services like delivery will be available also.
